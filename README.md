## How to Play

The rules are simple:
1. **Two players** - X (Purple) and O (Yellow)
2. **Take turns** - Tap empty squares to place your symbol
3. **Get three in a row** - Horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
4. **Win or draw** - First to get three in a row wins!
